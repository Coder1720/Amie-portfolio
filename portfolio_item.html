My First Python project

Coin Toss

This game needs no introduction but, Coin Toss is the practice of throwing a coin in the air and checking which side is showing when it lands, players must choose between two alternatives, heads or tails, sometimes used to resolve a dispute between two parties.

In this stimuation I make use of the random module to select between heads or tails. Two parameters are also set to allow the player to choose head or tails and also bet a certain amount of money. After both parameters have been set the player to told whether their guess is right and how much money they lost or gained.
